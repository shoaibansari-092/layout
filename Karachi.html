Karachi

Karachi is the largest and most populous city in Pakistan, located on the southern coast of the country. It is the capital of the Sindh province and serves as a major economic, industrial, and commercial center. As a bustling metropolis, Karachi is known for its vibrant energy, diverse population, and rich cultural heritage. The city has a mix of modern skyscrapers, colonial-era buildings, and traditional neighborhoods. It offers a blend of historical sites, cultural attractions, and a thriving urban lifestyle.

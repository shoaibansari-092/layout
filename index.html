Islamabad

Islamabad is the capital city of Pakistan. It is the most populous city in the Pakistan, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

Standing on the high mountain, Islamabad has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the two words islam and abad.
